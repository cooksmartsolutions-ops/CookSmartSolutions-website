About Us

Our commitment to smart tech.

Building better connections — inside, outside, and everywhere in between.

Cook Smart Solutions was founded by JD on a simple idea: high-end technology should feel effortless and just work. Based in Godley, TX and serving Johnson County and the surrounding North Texas communities — including Cleburne, Burleson, Joshua, Crowley, and Granbury — JD brings a personal, hands-on approach to every job.

After seeing too many residential and rural clients struggle with patchy Wi-Fi, mismatched tech, and contractors who disappeared after the install, JD built a service focused on clarity, reliability, and craft. From outdoor A/V and network infrastructure to phone repairs, smart home setup, and custom software — every job gets the same level of care.

The philosophy is simple: engineer for the environment, document for the future, and build like you'll be the one maintaining it a decade from now.

Reliability first

Systems designed to run for years, not months — with the right hardware for the environment.

Craft & care

Clean cable runs, documented installs, and attention to the details that separate "works" from "works beautifully."

Coverage everywhere

From indoor mesh Wi-Fi to long-range rural links — we design for the whole property, not just one room.

Tailored, always

Every project starts with understanding your goals, property, and budget — never a template.
